Sign in to DHSC Medicines and Reporting

Sign in to this portal to access Branded Medicines, Quarterly Collections and Reference Price

Branded Medicines

Use Branded Medicines to:

  • submit quarterly report sales
  • submit annual audited sales reports
  • submit annual unaudited presentation level data
  • provide the supporting documentation required for submission to the Department of Health and Social Care (DHSC) under both the Voluntary Scheme and Statutory Scheme

Quarterly Collections

Use Quarterly Collections to submit quarterly drug data to DHSC under the following schemes:

  • Generic medicines
  • Special medicines
  • Northern Ireland medicines
  • Category C medicines

Reference Price

Use the reference price portal to:

  • View and download data needed to calculate a reference price
  • View the reference price master data and audit logs

Before you start

You will need to install a multi-factor authenticator application to sign in securely.
